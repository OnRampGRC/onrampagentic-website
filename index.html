Built for Mid-Market

The Problem:

Your proposal team spends 31 hours on every RFP, chasing SMEs for answers and reformatting the same content over and over. Meanwhile, 20% of RFPs get abandoned because there's no time, and win rates hover at 45%.

The DealCoach Fix:

AI reads the RFP, pulls best-fit answers from your knowledge base, and drafts complete responses with citations. High-confidence answers auto-approve. Your team only reviews edge cases and gaps.

Intelligent extraction

Upload PDF, DOCX, or Excel RFPs. AI extracts every question, categorizes by type (Security, Technical, Pricing), and identifies response requirements automatically.

Knowledge retrieval

DealCoach searches your past proposals, security documentation, and product specs to find the best answers. Every response includes source citations for verification.

Gap detection

When information is missing—a pen test report, pricing approval, technical spec—DealCoach identifies the gap and routes to the appropriate SME for resolution.
